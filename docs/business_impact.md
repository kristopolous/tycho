# Tycho: The Archival-to-Social Monetization & Creator Engine
**From IMDb ID to Optimized Social Funnels and Influencer Clip Kits**

### The Opportunity: Unlocking the "Dark" Archive
Streaming platforms own thousands of hours of archival "Long Tail" content (e.g., *The Love Boat*, *Fantasy Island*). These assets are often "dark" because they lack modern, platform-optimized promotional material.
*   **The "Hidden Star" Hook:** These shows feature early-career appearances of today's A-list stars (e.g., Tom Cruise in 1979). 
*   **The Distribution Gap:** Fans are on TikTok, Instagram, and Threads, but there is no automated way to bridge the gap between a 40-year-old episode and a modern social feed.

### The Solution: Tycho
Tycho is a distribution-first engine that turns an IMDb `tt_id` into a suite of optimized social assets and **Creator-Ready Clip Kits** designed to drive subscriptions via both owned and organic channels.

1.  **IMDb-Driven Discovery:** You enter a `tt_id`. Tycho automatically fetches the full cast and headshots from IMDb.
2.  **Semantic Localization:** Using **TwelveLabs Marengo 3.0**, Tycho searches the video file using IMDb headshots as seeds to find the exact timestamps where those actors appear.
3.  **The "Best-Performing" Harness:** Tycho selects the specific **Promotional Harness** (template + tone + format) that has historically performed best for that specific **Talent/Platform pair** (e.g., a "Nostalgia" harness for TikTok).
4.  **Influencer & Creator Enablement:** Beyond finished ads, Tycho prepares curated **Clip Libraries**. These are high-quality, pre-cleared "Kits" sent to influencers and reviewers, enabling them to create organic "deep dive" or "review" content with ease.

### The Tycho Workflow: "One ID, Infinite Distribution"
*   **Input:** `tt_id` of an archival episode.
*   **Tycho Action:** Finds all guest stars; identifies the highest-performing harness for each.
*   **Outputs:** 
    *   **Direct Assets:** Ready-to-post TikToks, Instagram Reels, and Threads videos.
    *   **Creator Kits:** Curated clip libraries for organic influencer partnerships.
*   **Result:** A multi-channel funnel driving fans from social discovery directly to the archival asset on your platform.
