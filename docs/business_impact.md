# Tycho: Metadata-Driven Distribution & Optimization
**Matching Creative Harnesses to Distribution Channels for Maximum Conversion**

### The Core Problem: Static Creative in a Multi-Channel World
Media owners typically produce one trailer or one "catch-all" social post. But a "Nostalgia" strategy might work best for a veteran actor on Instagram, while a "High-Energy Action" harness might convert better for that same actor on TikTok.
*   **The Content Gap:** Archival libraries are static, but social feeds are dynamic. 
*   **The Optimization Gap:** Without automation, it's impossible to A/B test 10 different creative formulas across 5 different platforms for every actor in your library.

### The Solution: Tycho
Tycho is an **Optimization Engine** that decouples the *Discovery* of talent from the *Harness* used to package it for a specific *Channel*.

1.  **Talent Discovery:** Uses IMDb IDs and TwelveLabs AI to find every "Hidden Gem" moment in your archive.
2.  **Creative Harnesses:** Tycho provides a library of modular formulas (e.g., "Early Career Flashback", "Action Sizzle", "Deep Cut Interview"). 
3.  **Channel-Aware Packaging:** Each harness is optimized for the constraints of a specific **Distribution Channel** (TikTok, YouTube Shorts, Threads).
4.  **Convergence & Click Tracking (The Roadmap):** The architecture is designed to ingest performance metrics. Eventually, Tycho will automatically select the **Harness + Channel** pair that is historically proven to drive the highest engagement for that specific talent.

### ROI Scenario: The Tom Cruise '79 Campaign
**Goal:** Maximum conversion for a legacy TV show appearance.
*   **Variant A:** "Nostalgia" Harness on **Instagram** (1:1 aspect, warm VO).
*   **Variant B:** "Fast-Cut" Harness on **TikTok** (9:16 aspect, dramatic VO).
*   **Variant C:** "Documentary" Harness on **Threads** (RAW clips, trivia text).
*   **Result:** Tycho generates all three in one pass. The media manager tracks which funnel drives more platform subscriptions and doubles down on the winning Harness for that actor's entire filmography.

### Conclusion
Tycho turns your archive into a **high-fidelity marketing laboratory**. By automating the creation of multiple Harness/Channel variants, we ensure that every piece of archival content is working at its peak conversion potential.
